The Journey

The Journey is a new gamemode in FIFA 17. The Journey lets you make your mark on and off the pitch as Alex Hunter, a rising star in the Premier League.


Career Mode

Career mode is a gamemode which allows you to manage a team and buy players around the world.
It also allows you to make a player and play matches and train him throughout his career.

Ultimate team

Throughout 5 years Ultimate Team is the most used gamemode in FIFA. It allows you to create your Ultimate Team, Buy your favorite players.

You can them play with them against random people online. You want to win each game so then you can move through seasons. You start in season 10 which is the worst but you want to make it to season 1. After winning or promoting to a new season you will recieve rewards. In these rewards you could get the coins to buy the best players in the world.
